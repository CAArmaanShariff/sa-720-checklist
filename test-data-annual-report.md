# MANAGEMENT DISCUSSION AND ANALYSIS

## Industry Structure and Developments

The Company operates in the manufacturing sector with a focus on precision engineering components.

## Financial Performance

### Revenue Analysis
The Company recorded **revenue of ₹12,800 Lakhs** during FY 2025-26, representing a year-on-year growth of 25.5%. This growth was driven by increased demand from automotive and industrial sectors.

### Profitability
**Net Profit after Tax stood at ₹1,180 Lakhs** for the year, compared to ₹758 Lakhs in the previous year. The profit margin improved to approximately 9.2%.

### Borrowings
As at March 31, 2026, the Company's debt portfolio stands at:
- **Long-term borrowings: ₹2,500 Lakhs**
- Interest rate: 9.5% per annum

The **Debt-Equity Ratio has improved to 0.65:1** from 0.85:1 in the previous year, reflecting prudent financial management.

### Key Financial Metrics

| Metric | FY 2025-26 | FY 2024-25 | Change |
|--------|------------|------------|--------|
| Revenue | ₹12,800 Lakhs | ₹10,200 Lakhs | +25.5% |
| Net Profit | ₹1,180 Lakhs | ₹758 Lakhs | +55.7% |
| Total Equity | ₹3,750 Lakhs | ₹2,580 Lakhs | +45.3% |
| Debt-Equity Ratio | 0.67:1 | 0.85:1 | Improved |
| Current Ratio | 1.55:1 | 1.51:1 | Stable |

**Note: Deleveraging efforts have significantly improved the Debt-Equity ratio from 0.85 to 0.67.**
